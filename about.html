About Us

Our company is dedicated to enhancing vehicles through innovative styling and technology. For over 50 years, we have partnered with top automakers to create striking designs that redefine what an automobile can be. Our team of talented designers brings a keen eye for aesthetics and an understanding of the latest trends. We collaborate closely with manufacturers to develop exteriors that are both head-turning and aerodynamic. But our work goes beyond skin deep. Through rigorous testing and engineering, we also improve how a vehicle handles, rides, and performs. Our Performance division helps tune suspension, steering, and braking systems to provide an unparalleled driving experience. We utilize cutting-edge software and hardware to extract every ounce of power from an engine. And our work extends under the hood through advancements in fuel delivery, combustion, and more. At the end of the day, our goal is simple: create cars that look, feel and drive better than anything else on the road. Automakers and drivers around the world turn to us when they want to push the limits of style and performance.
